02

Typography

Mona Sans is the main font across the design system. Source Serif 4 is available as an editorial highlight for moments that need extra craft, warmth, or premium texture.

Default: Mona Sans

Helping teams turn signals into decisions.

Mona Sans is the everyday voice for UI, landing pages, product surfaces, navigation, labels, and body copy. It should carry most of the system because it is clear, structured, and easy to scan.

Editorial option: Source Serif 4

Use Source Serif 4 at 800 for selected brand moments.

Use Source Serif 4 Italic at 500 for pull quotes and editorial accents.

03

Logos

The logo system covers Leaf, service sub logos, and special properties like the Blog and Colectivo podcast. Service and property marks now ship as padded and no-padding exports, so choose the version that matches the layout instead of cropping by hand.

Safe margin

Keep the built-in frame.

Padded exports carry their own clear area. The margin around the visible artwork equals half the Leaf icon, which keeps the family consistent when logos move across product, marketing, sales, and internal materials.

Use padded exports

For shared files, loose canvases, external use, and placements where the logo needs to carry its own safe area.

Use no-padding exports

For application UI, cards, navigation, watermarks, and layouts where the surrounding component already controls spacing.

Do not crop

If a padded file feels visually off in a component, switch to the matching no-padding export instead of trimming the asset.

Colour with icons

Secondary colour can group content without changing the icon language.

Use black SVG icons over secondary colour fields for page libraries, learning hubs, onboarding collections, and editorial navigation. Keep labels on light surfaces so the colour does the grouping and Mona carries the content.
